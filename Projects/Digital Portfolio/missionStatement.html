Personal Mission Statement

"I am going to reach for the stars, no matter how far"


★ Star 1: "I understand and respect different perspectives."

  • -   I actively listen to others without distraction. When others speak, I strictly listen without interrupting. When I respond or question, I do so with respect and kindness.
  • -   When my views clash with others, I first make sure to fully understand the opposing view before presenting my own.
  • -   I accept individuals regardless of their ethnicity, race, culture, religion, disabilities, and sexual orientation.
  • -   I provide a resting shoulder, at any point of time.
  • -   I do not put others down for their mistakes. Rather I look at the issue and search for solutions.
  • -   I am a team player who works collaboratively and values the presence of every individual.
  • -   I do not turn a blind eye to someone in need.
  • -   I do not provide support expecting something in return. If the act is reciprocated, I will be appreciative.

★ Star 3: "I maintain my calm and collectedness, even in the face of turbulent emotions."

  • -   When I am upset, stressed, angry or sad, I can communicate appropriately without passing on my frustrations to others.
  • -   No matter how happy, ecstatic, or successful, I will not be overconfident with my abilities. The opportunities of improvement are always present.
  • -   When fearful, nervous, anxious, or hopeless, I reflect on the causations and work towards building confidence in these areas. I put myself in uncomfortable situations to better face such emotions.

★ Star 4: "I take care of my physical and mental wellbeing."

  • -   I regularly exercise to maintain good fitness levels.
  • -   I am conscious of the food and drinks I consume and eliminate harmful items.
  • -   I get consistent 7-8 hours of sleep.
  • -   I practice self-reflection to understand and improve my being.
  • -   I practice mindfulness to be more present in the now.

★ Star 5: "I am proficient with full stack development."

  • -   I can solve all technical issues on every level of the stack.
  • -   I can effectively analyse and implement programs with minimal time and space complexity.
  • -   I can identify and implement design patterns when most appropriate.
  • -   I ensure that my code is readable and maintainable.
  • -   I minimise coupling and design smells.
  • -   I ensure I meet client requirements.

★ Star 6: "I am an expert in the field of Artificial Intelligence."

  • -   I can comfortably work with diverse data and learning models.
  • -   I am a driver of implementing AI solutions at the workplace.
  • -   I ensure that the data being worked with has not drifted.
  • -   I maintain the highest level of integrity and trust when working with consumer data.
  • -   I understand the ethical considerations and regulatory compliance before the decision-making process.

★ Star 7: "I am a successful author."

  • -   I write fantasy novels which reflect my creative mind.
  • -   I bring my characters to life. I do so by understanding their emotions and perspectives.
  • -   I do thorough research of the world setting.
  • -   I thoroughly plan the events of the story.

★ Star 8: "I am a passionate video creator."

  • -   I create entertaining content for my viewers.
  • -   I learn new video editing technique with each new video.
  • -   I focus on quality over quantity.

★ Star 9: "I have the mindset of a student at every instance of life."

  • -   I am always learning about new technological innovations.
  • -   I reflect on weaknesses pointed out by others and discovered through self-reflection. I then work towards, translating these weaknesses into strengths.
  • -   I read daily to expand my perspectives and knowledge.

★ Star 10: "I find time for my loved ones and personal hobbies."

  • -   I make time for my beautiful and fantastic girlfriend.
  • -   I find time for my friends and family.
  • -   I enjoy and play video games.
  • -   I watch anime and retain my connection to Japan.
  • -   I regularly play table tennis which is the sport I love the most.

★ Star 11: "I am grateful and appreciative of life."

  • -   I understand and value the little things.
  • -   I see my failures as learning experiences.
  • -   I never complain about my current circumstances.
